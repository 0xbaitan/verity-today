Ingredients:-
4 cloves Garlic
½ Big Onion
2 tbsp Spring onion
1 tsp Red chilli
1 tbsp Chilli paste
1 tbsp Char sui sauce
250gm Pad Thai noodles
1.5 tbsp Dark Soy

Method:-
Add garlic, onions, and red chilli to the oil. Then add char Sui, Soy, and chill sauce. Add noodles and toss well. Garnish with spring onions

What exactly is the Contactless Safe Hand Wash Station?

The Contactless Safe Hand Wash Station is a hands-free, foot-operated, compact, durable handwashing solution suitable for residential, commercial, industrial, medical, and public spaces. It is not only easy to assemble, transport, and maintain, but also has a sleek, compact design that makes it space-saving.

Tell us about how this project got started.

We are already living in a VUCA (Volatile, Uncertain, Complex, Ambiguous) world and COVID-19 has shaken up our very way of existing, locally and globally. It was the need of the hour to be prepared for a post-lockdown new normal. Handwashing is crucial to winning this battle and our focus was on creating an efficient, safe, cost-effective solution that could be easily adopted by multiple sectors and industries.
Some of our noteworthy customers are the Indian Army, Indian Navy (INS Netaji Subhas), West Bengal State Agricultural Marketing Board, Kolkata Port Trust, Saroj Gupta Cancer Centre & Research Institute, Thakurpukur, Medica Superspecialty Hospital, Indian Institute of Cerebral Palsy (IICP), Royal Calcutta Golf Club (RCGC), Vikram Solar, and more.

What was the product development process?

After testing multiple prototypes, two variations of the first model were launched – the Regular Single Basin (200 Litre Tank Capacity) and the Ultra Single Basin (500 Litre Tank Capacity). The overwhelmingly positive response to these and further R&D led to the design of a second model that is a sanitisation must-have for places with high footfall. Designed with both safe & efficient handwashing in mind, this model - Contactless Safe Wash Station 2.0 Deluxe Dual - has two foldable, splash-free basins that allow for two people to safely and simultaneously wash their hands.
For all models, the 200-litre tank allows for up to 300 hand washes per refill while the 500-litre tank allows for up to 750 hand washes per refill. Available in black, blue, and green, the water tanks are made of 100% virgin food-grade plastic. The stand on which the tank is placed is made of powder coated metal, making it rust-proof.

Tell us a little about the family business, Patton Group.

PATTON is a government-recognised export house and an ISO 9001:2015 company that manufactures and exports premiere EMT Conduit Fittings to USA Canada, Mexico, Germany, etc. We are also a leader in the field of Plastic Tanks, Crates, Bins and PVC pipes, with 6 sophisticated state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities at various locations in West Bengal. PATTON is a consecutive winner of Productivity & National Awards for Excellence in Exports.
