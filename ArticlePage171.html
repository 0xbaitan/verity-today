 ONE PLUS 8T


Gurmehr Bindra

Dec 1, 2020   |   Technology



Background

It was customary for One Plus to release a smartphone belonging to the 'T' series. These smartphones are rather more advanced than their counterpart released earlier in the year. These smartphones are based on customer feedback and are intended to be relevant till the new generation of One Plus smartphones settle in next year.
The ONE PLUS 8T was released on 14th October 2020. The company made much effort to highlight the ONE PLUS 8 Pro and make it more and more appealing. Thus, they sidelined the original non pro version of One Plus 8 released at about the same time as the Pro. The 8T is expected to attract customers and catch their eye on non pro versions of their smartphones
Let's look into some details and specifications of ONE PLUS 8T and see if it's worth buying Price

It starts at ₹42999 in India, $749 in USA and £549 in Great Britain. One Plus is known to have smartphones at an affordable price range at this is no exception.
DESIGN AND Software

The One Plus 8T comes in aquamarine green and lunar silver colours. It has a screen size of 6.55 inches and resolution of 1080×2400 pixels. Thus, pixels per inch being 402. It weighs 188g which isn't too heavy and has a battery capacity of 4500 mAh.
A few notable upgrades which have been made are-

  • 1) A 120Hz display refresh rate
  • 2) 65W wired charging instead of 30W
  • 3) A quad camera system instead of the triple cameras



It uses the same processor as the 8Pro which is the Qualcomm Snapdragon 865. It has a 12GB RAM and an internal storage capacity of 256 GB The Operating System is Android 11 which means you don't need to upgrade the software. It uses the Oxygen OS 11 software for Skin. Face unlock and fingerprint sensors are both present. Along with that it has a compass, proximity sensor, accelometer, ambient light sensor and a gyroscope However, One Plus doesn't have the best history with camera functionality and they do not offer the best cameras. The rear camera is 48-megapixel(f/1.7) + 16 megapixel (f/2.2) + 5 megapixel + 2 megapixel Front camera is 16 megapixel (f/2.4) Overall One Plus 8T is a good choice. It has upgraded features and a wide range of advanced and selective options. However, a few specifications they missed out on which other smartphones can offer are- Wireless charging An IP rating against water and dust The fastest processor available Tons of internal storage and the ability to expand it A better quality camera This however does not underestimate the power of this smartphone which is recommended for those who are on the search for an affordable phone which is easy to handle and does not give too many problems. One Plus 8T has been reviewed to be better than the One Plus 8 Pro and if you are on a lookout for a perfect phone from the brand you should buy it.
