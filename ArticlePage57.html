 DESI PARDESI


Srishti Banerjee

August 1, 2020   |   Creativity

Virat Kohli and Anushka Sharma, Ranvir Singh and Deepika Padukone– both the couples got married in Italy. There's no doubt that they are perfect for each other, but the one thing that might not have been perfect is their wedding menu. Are you wondering why? It's obvious! They didn't have an Indo-Italian fusion Pizza at their wedding! And come on, all an Indian attending a wedding cares about is food!



Now, we can't have you make the same mistake when you marry your Dulhan/Dulheraja, can we? So we prepared a fusion for you– Tikka Pizza with a Makhani base. A perfect blend of the Italian pizza recipe served with our desi chicken tikka makhani base. Feel free to try this, take a picture and tag us @verity.today!

Recipe for the base:

Ingredients:
1 cup all-purpose flour
1½ cup yoghurt
2 tablespoons of olive oil
¼ teaspoon baking soda
A pinch of salt

Procedure:
In a bowl, add flour, oil, baking soda, salt and mix it well.
Add the yoghurt gradually till a glutenous mixture is formed. The dough must be wet and sticky.
Cover it for 30 mins with a damp cloth.
After 30mins you'll have a risen dough. Knead it for 5 more minutes.
Divide the dough in half and flatten it by hand. Prick the base with a fork so that it doesn't fluff.
Toast one side of the base on a Tawa, flip it over and set it aside.

Recipe for the Makhana Sauce:
Ingredients:
200gms of tomatoes, chopped
1 medium-sized onion, finely chopped.
1 cup of water
5-6 garlic cloves
100gms butter
10-12 cashews
2 teaspoons of red chilli powder
1 teaspoon Garam Masala powder
1 teaspoon of Kasuri methi
1 tablespoon of honey
Salt to taste.

Procedure:
In a saucepan, add 30 gms butter( approx. 2 tablespoons), tomatoes, garlic, onions and cashews. Sauté well. Add ½ cup of water and cook till the tomatoes are soft.
Pour the mixture into a blender and grind it into a smooth paste. Pass it through a sieve.
In another pan, add the pureed mixture, the rest of the butter and the honey. Cook for 20mins till you get a smooth sauce. Add the Kasuri methi. Reduce it to half and let it cool.

Recipe for the chicken tikka (topping):

Ingredients:
100 gms chicken thighs
½ cup hung curd
1 tablespoon of garam masala
1 tablespoon of red chilli powder
1 tablespoon of jeera powder
1 teaspoon of Kasuri methi
2 tablespoons of mustard oil
2 tablespoons of ghee/butter
1 tablespoon of oil (regular vegetable oil)
Salt to taste.

Procedure:
Cut the chicken thighs into bite-sized pieces. In a bowl, marinate chicken with curd and all the spices. Add the mustard oil and leave it in the refrigerator for 30 mins to 1 hour. In a pan, add the ghee and oil. Fry the chicken pieces till they char. Set them aside.

Assembly:
On the base, add half the sauce and spread it evenly. Add the chicken tikka, some bell peppers, onions and then go wild with toppings! Take some processed cheese, some mozzarella and add as much as you want.
Heat up a frying pan and carefully put the uncooked pizza on it.
Cover it and put the stove on medium-low heat. Wait for 20-25 mins, till the cheese melts.
Slice and serve hot!
