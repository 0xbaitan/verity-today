Hi! We are Verity Today

    Verity Today is a student-led publication aimed at providing "something for everyone". Founded by Sohani Wahi, the team has expanded into an organisation that organises regular events in conjunction with the newsletter itself. We aim to empower our readers in an era where the newspaper has gradually declined. Information is more valuable than before, and awareness is essential in modernity: Verity Today is here to mobilise a revolution.

    Originally consisting of ten departments, we usher in a new era of Verity 2.0 with six departments and smaller albeit close-knit teams, emphasising quality over quantity.
