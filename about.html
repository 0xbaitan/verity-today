Hi! We are Verity Today






Verity Today is an online based newspaper and newsletter that is especially targets the new, upcoming generation. It was founded by Sohani Wahi a 10th grade student and currently the team stands strong with a strength of 100+ students from different age groups. Verity Today plans to create a forum where opinions and views can be expressed without any harsh counter backs. Given the coming times, the rate of newspaper reading has gone down immensely, however it is vital to stay updated. Verity wished to provide this basic information to our readers in a way as to interest them.

We hope to make this a very interactive forum for you all by asking for your inputs and suggestions on what might interest you so that you too, can have something which in a way is yours too.

One main motive/slogan is “a little something for everyone” . We want something in our newsletter of interest to everyone, thus bringing us to our wide range of departments.

Verity was merely, just a thought which turned into a reality. It was inspired by different student led organisations that rook over our thoughts and mesmerised us, wanting us to start something of our own. .

Not only does Verity provide information regarding affairs on important subjects like global affairs and business but we also have an entertainment and creativity departments for you to enjoy as well as support growing artists.
