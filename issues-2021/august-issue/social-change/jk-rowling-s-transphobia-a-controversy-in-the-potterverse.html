  JK Rowling's Transphobia: A controversy in the Potterverse


Vidushi

August 31, 2021   |   Social Change


The wizarding world- the land where magic comes true- is a realm full of stories! It is a safe place for people from all the different communities to relish the blissful days of when they'd dream of their first Hogwarts letter, How they learned to pronounce "wingardium leviOsa", and "Alohomora". Everyone would find comfort in this fictional world.



However, recently, the famous author- JK Rowling- has been impeached for her transphobic tweets that have caused tremendous distress to the trans community and its allies who celebrate the power of pride and magic by dressing up as characters from the fictional universe. Hogwarts- The world of Harry Potter- where magic comes alive consists of all the stories that teach us how one should not be discriminated against for their identity!

Last year, JK Rowling was accused and called out for being transphobic. She had written tweets and articles implying that a person's 'sex' is circumscribed by biology and should be protected by law- completely disregarding gender identity! The writer argued that women's rights are being 'eroded' by the inclusion of transwomen in women-friendly spaces- hence elevating transphobia. Rowling has frequently shown her less than accommodating views on trans people. This adds to transphobes feeling that their toxic ideas are now backed up and that trans-exclusionists are 'sustained.'

JK Rowling is entitled to have her opinions and her freedom of speech, however, these judgments and evaluations are targeted towards one specific society- labelling them as predators who invade women-safe spaces like public restrooms and changing rooms. The feelings of transwomen are invalidated- resulting in a large number of people reconsidering their gender identity!

JK Rowling is a multi-national bestselling author, needless to say, that her creativity and intelligence is beyond the world! It is also great, as a matter of fact, it's splendid!-to be actively engaged in the world of Harry Potter! Because magic is the legacy we carry with ourselves, it is something that we- as readers- cherish forever! Plenty of Queer writers like- Virginia Woolf and James Baldwin have been celebrating and supporting the presence of LGBTQ+ folks, overcoming the darkest times of oppression and harassment against them. Presently, there are a large number of Queer writers, poets and content creators who speak up against the melancholy on a daily basis. These Queer writers require our support as a community; it is a great way to spread kindness while appreciating the pleasantest!

Lastly, It is necessary to understand that when a person reflects their beliefs towards an individual or a community of the society- it is significant to consider that their sentiments do not overline with your privilege. Creating a harmless, reliable and secure space for individuals of all communities to express themselves as who they are is extremely necessary! Every once in a while, when someone introspects- so much has changed- and change can be beneficial too! Maybe we can all unlearn the wrong in this world and support individuals in their truest-happiest form!

After all, It's only human to serve ourselves without hatred, violence and discrimination.
