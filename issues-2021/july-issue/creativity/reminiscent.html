 Reminiscent


Ishani Datta

July 18, 2021   |   Creativity




5am train ride,
i wanted to see the sun rise
to my dismay,
the rain pelted down the railway
it was grey,
the lightning struck, i recoiled away
the lightning always scared me,
until you hugged me and said don’t worry
it’s all blurry, i’m here dont worry
at 13 i wondered if we were for sure
or i’m stupid and this would backfire
i’ll forever remember the way
you sprinted down the pathway
it was 5 in the evening
i could’ve stayed till the next day
truth be told,
i just wanted to know you were there
all i knew was your laughter
your smile, your eyes, your banter
i could stay till next winter
7 years and 9,000 miles
plenty of time to wonder why
it took 20 years to say
‘i love you don’t go please stay’
the train rolls into my hometown
i get off and look around
everything’s the same, just
wish you were here anyway
i wanted to see how you were
but you didn’t want to remember
what we once were together
but i know one day
when it rains and you shiver,
you’ll run back here and say
i should’ve stayed longer
when your thrill expires
fixing your messy truck tires
you’ll remember me you’d pray
i didn’t get away
if you told me from the start
9000 miles wouldn’t be far apart
