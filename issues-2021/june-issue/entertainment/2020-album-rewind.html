 2020 Album Rewind


Dia Marik

May 1, 2021   |   Entertainment


Happy New Year everyone! Feels crazy that we’re out of that very apocalyptic year of 2020, no guarantee that the one we are in right now will be any less apocalyptic so I shall not keep my hopes up too high. Now 2020 practically defeated our expectations in every way possible. Well, actually, it exceeded them in so many wrong ways, it’s just sad. But one sphere where 2020 managed to not lack in was the music sector. It was actually pretty good. Comparing my 2020 “Spotify Wrapped” to my 2019 one made me feel better about my music taste as I had pretty much opened up my taste buds for all sorts of genres. As I said, since 2020 managed to satisfy my musical needs I shall be talking about some of the albums that came out in 2020 that basically kept me going back then and still are, in 2021.

The first album I will be talking about is Heaven or Hell by Don Toliver. I haven’t heard too many people talk about it but I bet almost all of us have heard two songs from this album- After Party and No Idea. I think Don Toliver is quite underrated but he’s slowly getting the recognition he deserves. The album is basically about love and all the songs go really hard. I swear the songs from the album were the only ones I had to repeat throughout the early lockdown days, giving me a bit of nostalgia. So if you’re into RnB and rap music, I would recommend this album. Some of my favourite tracks that are also underrated are Euphoria featuring Kash Paige, Travis Scott and Wasted.

Next album is Eternal Atake by Lil Uzi Vert (the deluxe version) I think everyone who likes rap music was really looking forward to this album, and it did not disappoint. I really thought it would attain the milestone of Album of The Year. In this album Lil Uzi Vert brought together many artists like 21 Savage, Chief Keef, Future, Gunna, NAV etc. producing 32 songs, almost all of them enjoyable. I honestly never knew so much about Lil Uzi Vert but this album really changed everything and he’s now one of my top artists. Some of my favourite songs from the album are- Silly Watch, Yessirskiii, Futsal Shuffle 2020, That Way and Myron.



Another album that came out in 2020 that I’ll be talking about is After Hours by The Weeknd. I don’t think there’s anyone who can dislike The Weeknd, his music has been good throughout his years of scrutinized stardom. Though I did see some people complain that After Hours was good but not up to the mark, compared to his other works, I thought it was amazing and also slightly different. The album has got some really good songs, which I think everyone enjoyed, no matter what genre they’re into. I also doubt there’s anyone who hasn’t heard a single song from this album. Among all the songs from the album my personal favourites are Sacred to Live, In Your Eyes, Faith and After Hours. Blinding Lights has been dominating the charts since it’s release.



The last album that I want to talk about is probably an album that saved 2020, which I never really thought was my type. Nevertheless, I still thoroughly enjoyed it. Positions by Ariana Grande was dropped in late 2020 and of course, the whole world started playing it in no time. In this album there are many cheesy romantic tracks, all of them being very enjoyable music. I didn’t think I was going to really vibe to it but I was wrong, I really liked the album, it was indeed a game changer for me. Some of my favourite songs from the album were pov, motive(with Doja Cat), my hair, and 34+35.
