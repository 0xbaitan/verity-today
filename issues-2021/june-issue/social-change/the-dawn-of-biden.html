 The Dawn of Biden


Rajkishore Mukherjee

May 1, 2021   |   Social Change


The US Presidential Elections have paved the way for a new change, especially looking at the expectations that the people of America have been holding right from a year before its commencement.



To predict that Biden would be a better President than Trump, right within two months of his incumbency would be rather unfair, although, seemingly positive reactions to his policies are abundant. Biden has put a lot of hope within the American citizens and, the thought of a new change and a new life would intrigue them. To enunciate upon this, I would be breaking the article into two parts and we would take within a discussion about Biden and the reforms which he has already introduced to bring forward a new change with the state such as rejoining the Paris Agreement and the increase in corporate taxes.

The point that I would love to elucidate upon is that America needs change. We need to realise that a change in one of the most dominant nations in the world will have a domino effect on the rest of the world, be it positively or negatively and we need to understand the importance of that. Biden carries that new beacon of hope, especially after the tumultuous years in US throughout the tenure of 2016-2020. Tyranny, at its best.

Biden has paved a foundation for hope, and whether he would cater and keep up to his promises is yet to be known. I applaud these policies because they are very important stepping stones towards instilling a new hope within the people of the US, even for the people who haven't had their firm belief within Biden.

Biden has pledged to work for everyone - such is his wholehearted personality.
