 Gaming Phones and their Impact on the Smartphone Industry


Gurmehr Bindra

June 1, 2021   |   Technology


The smartphone industry is probably one of the most rapidly developing industries at the present moment and gaming is an aspect which merits a fair share of credit for this development. Mobile Gaming is gaining popularity all over the world simply due to convenience and easy accessibility. A few companies accept that this might turn out to be the eventual fate of gaming. Most games available on PCs or consoles (PS, Xbox etc) can be played on mobile phones for barely a portion of the initial cost.



Taking a gander from the gamers point of view a few common smartphones do not match the required specs to easily play advanced games. A higher frame rate, graphic intensity and stronger hardware is required to smoothly runs games requiring greater standards of performance such as Call of Duty: Mobile, Asphalt, Fortnite and PUBG. This paves the way for the origin of a gaming phone to provide the best experience out of these games.

The best gaming smartphones are prepared according to the requirements for intensive gaming. These include extraordinarily turned processors, a support for gaming accessories, loads of RAM and a fast storage, vibrant display that has a high refresh rate, long battery life and importantly a fast touch response. Fundamentally it’s all that you need in a smartphone, with the features beefed up.

There’s nothing new about the fact that all smartphones have gaming capabilities and many of them have really suitable specs as well. Yet, why do we see a rise in purchase of specific gaming phones like Asus, Black Shark and Lenovo Legion when there are other phones (OnePlus, Mi flagships and Samsung flagships) with similar capabilities.

The answer to this is the vibe which these gaming phones give off. The design of a gaming phone is different and it is accommodated with an RGB strip to provide you with an aesthetic gaming energy.

The gaming capability is what these gamers take into account when they buy phones and most of these phones are capable of providing the level of performance which they search for. Due to the rapid rise of mobile gamers, certain companies (Asus, Black Shark and Lenovo) have focused on releasing phones to appeal to these gamers and this strategy of theirs is definitely selling very well.

Comparing an Asus Rog 5 to a one plus 9, one may notice that Asus is slightly better in terms of game performance. It has a few software improvements such as upgrades inbuilt coolers and ultrasonic buttons with 9 sensors to offer enhanced gaming capabilities. However, the ASUS Rog 9 is considered to be much more, and is appealing in a gamers perspective since they are branded to be specifically designed for gaming. They provide that extra boost in the experience which contributes towards their widespread popularity.

The gaming phone has had a significant impact on the smartphone industry by providing a completely new outlook to marketing. A high share of revenue in gaming indicates the interests and viewpoints the buyers have. Every gaming phone must have three important features which distinguishes it from the rest. These are-

-High refresh rate and resolution
-RAM above 6Gb
-A superior, advanced and powerful chipset

These gaming phones have a very bright future with growing interest in mobile gaming. Yet, with a number of genuine smartphones coming up with features and performance levels similar to a gaming phone, greater efforts must be put in to separate them (advancement wise) from the rest of the competition.
