 A Gilmpse Into The Air Pods Max


Vedant Mohata

May 1, 2021   |   Technology


$549. The first thing that comes to mind when someone mentions the AirPods Max. The second thing that comes to mind is that these ultra-expensive headphones still cost $150 less than Apple's wheels for the Mac Pro. The price wasn’t high enough though, to stop people from buying these headphones, as they sold out in all 5 gorgeous colors worldwide.



The AirPods Max is surprisingly comfortable for their weight and has a build quality that is much better than the other headphones at this price range. The AirPods Max does sound impressive, like high-end headphones, with tight bass, natural mids, crisp highs, and a wide soundstage for a closed-back headphone. Apple does have EQ settings (under Music in Settings) - for Apple Music anyway -- and you can make some slight customizations to the sound profile.

The smart case provided for the headphones does look good, but it won’t help much in protecting them in case one ever drops this ultra-expensive way to listen to AC/DC. Apple says that these headphones last 20 hours, at 80% volume, which is honestly quite impressive.

Like the AirPods Pro, the AirPods max support spatial audio and have an insane number of microphones(9).

All in all, AirPods Max is one of the best headphones money can buy, but not worth the tag price of 549$.
