    1. Memoroids

    Shivangi Biswas started Memoroids- a hub for Polaroids prints on film, frames, Polaroid holders, gift hampers, on 13th May, 2020 when she felt that school demotivated her and made her feel that she wasn’t worth something. We all need something to hold onto on our worst days for Shivangi it was this, when her friend motivated her to start this venture and she feels confident now more than ever. She has made round about 90 sales and counting and invests in an enjoyable unboxing experience for her customers, while keeping packing simple but cute and colour coordinated. Her secret to motivation when there are no orders placed is “Knowing that no matter what happens, I am performing to my full and utmost capacity and that I should proud of myself for whatever I’ve achieved, no matter how little or how less.” Her hard work on frames and wooden polaroid is very much visible when it’s a finished work, and I’m sure will match most of our aesthetics. However, it’s not always roses and sunflowers for her either. She faces some very common problems like rude customers, ghosting, and bargaining, finances, not enough orders but at the end of the day she said, “We should be proud of ourselves for doing what we are and give ourselves a pat on our backs for coming this far.”

    2. Cakewalk by Rahas

    The Raha sisters, Nandini and Shreyanshi started this home bakery to serve “melt in mouth, perfectly balanced cakes” on 18th January, 2021. The baker duo of Raha family was always fond of cooking and baking, and received immense amount of support from family and friends, but never did the thought of turning a passion into a business start-up crossed their mind until recently when they noticed that people switched to home-run bakeries which according to them was safer during the ongoing pandemic. Their menu allows us to explore a wide range of cakes and cookies, and their bestsellers are lemon and strawberry cake, but you can also try brownies, brownie cake pop, double chocolate cookies and granola bars and oatmeal cookies are a special item for fitness freaks, with 80 successful orders the duo is all set to treat your taste buds. To pack the order in the post elegant and hygienic order, they make sure to follow all sanitation process making the unboxing experience a mouth-watering one. With work for one and studies for another surely running a bakery isn’t easy because it’s not a two minutes job but requires a large amount of patience, to keep up with motivation “The days I don't get an order I usually cook a meal for myself and binge watch a series on Netflix.” said Nandini. As we all know that a coin has two sides, similarly they face some trouble with continuous price negotiations and dealing with too many orders but one equipment for making, but nonetheless it does not stop the duo from growing and learning.

    3. Rooh and Flowers

    “Have you heard of bees getting stuck in amber and being preserved for ages? Leaves frozen in snow? The work I do is almost the same, except it's in a form you can keep close to your heart, forever.” Is how Rajshree introduced her small business, which started sometime in October,2020 at 2:00am when she sat with a bunch of pressed flowers, a bottle of memories and tons of memories she wasn’t ready to let go of. What was merely started as a resin botanical jewellery store has now expanded to resin home décor and curated charm jewellery, and has received around about 900+ orders. Most of the items are resin made and need to be dealt with a lot of patience, while finishing really gives birth to a yearn for such beauty hand-made with love. Rajshree on being asked about the speciality of her packaging said, “The 'thank you letter' I add isn't just a letter appreciating the purchase, it's a letter from me to you, with LOTS OF LOVE” and from personal experience reading the letter does provide one with a sense of warmth and belonging to a clan.

    4. Oxygreen: Plants and Décor

    Shalini’s love for plants is beyond any measure and she wanted to share this love and experience with others as well, so she asked her daughter Teena to help her setup a business account for the same in July, 2021. The business is managed by Shalini and her two daughters; they extend variety in plants and planters- ceramic and terracotta which undeniably attracts buyers appreciating nature and promises more calm and peaceful environment. With 25-30 orders, the green family isn’t demotivate but understand that their business will reach its peak even those hurdles will create disturbances, “It's normal to not get orders every day, we just keep focusing on our plants to keep ourselves motivated. The love for taking care of plants is why we started this business and plants don't grow in one day, it takes time for steady growth” said Teena. The thing that charms customers is the 100% green and eco-friendly packaging, which underlines the need for less pollution in whatever way possible.

    5. Ayda by Ayesha

    How often do we come across business owners who don’t believe in sugar-coating, well Ayesha is one of those. She started her venture inspired by others small businesses, and also desired to showcase her creativity by putting into some productive use. Ayesha is definitely filled with a lot of life, when asked about her motivational secret she said- “I have this craZe of earning money. That is one thing that keeps me going.” She has received 40 orders and deals in a variety of goods, starting from premium quality leather products, vintage shirts and handmade jewellery and phone charms using beads and pearls. A very important positive side of owning a small business is that it is not supposed to leave you to deal with stress and sleepless nights but let you enjoy through the process, something Ayesha experiences “I used colourful sugar beads to make rings, anklets, bracelets, chokers and necklaces. I also make bracelets and anklets out of embroidery threads which definitely reminds me of my school days.” Alongside the happiness, when potential customers ghost after a detailed conversation for products it is disappointing in addition to users unfollowing over a period of time. The small business radiates life and optimistic vibes just like a sun shining in the morning.
